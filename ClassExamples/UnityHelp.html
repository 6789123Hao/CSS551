Begin working with Unity

Installation, the editor, and tutorials, go over this yourself after class:

  • Get started with Unity Hub
  • Manual pages: Overview. You MUST look at
    • how to use the editor.
    • If you like/have time: you can look at details of the edtior features (I don't know most of these, and I am surviving).
  • After you know what the editor is about, take a look at https://learn.unity.com/tutorials:
    • Unity Essentials
    • Take a veryquick look at: Junior Programmer (the first or two sessions would be more than enough)
    • Remember, you are not learning how to build a game, rather, you want to be able to use Unity, that's all.
